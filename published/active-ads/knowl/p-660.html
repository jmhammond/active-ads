  1. Determine the orders of different elements of the group \(\mathbb{Z}_{5}\text{.}\)
  2. Determine the orders of different elements of the group \(\mathbb{Z}_{15}\text{.}\)
  3. Determine the orders of different elements of the group \(\mathbb{Z}_{16}\text{.}\)
  4. Comparing the results you got above, hypothesize on what you would expect for different moduli
