Suppose \([G;*]\) is a finite group of order \(n\text{.}\) The last theorem in Section 11.3 in Section 10.3 states that if \(a\in G\text{,}\) there exists a positive integer, \(m\) less than \(n\) such that \(a^m=e\text{.}\) Prove that the least such positive integer is a divisor of \(n\text{.}\)
