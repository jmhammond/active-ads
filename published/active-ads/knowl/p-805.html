Given a graph with vertex set \(\{1, 2, 3, 4, 5\}\text{,}\) the group of automorphisms of that graph will be a subgroup of the group \(S_4\text{.}\) For each of the following subgroups of \(S_5\text{,}\) draw a graph (directed or undirected) that has that subgroup as its automorphism group.
  1. \(\displaystyle \{i\}\)
  2. The cyclic subgroup generated by \((1,2)\)
  3. The cyclic subgroup generated by \((1,2,3,4,5)\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \{\sigma \in S_5 \mid \sigma(5)=5 \}\)
