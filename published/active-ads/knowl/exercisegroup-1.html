4.
An even number is an (integer) multiple of 2. For example, 12 is even because \(12 = 6 \cdot 2\) but 13 is not even since \(12 = \frac{13}{2} \cdot 2\text{.}\) How can you quickly tell whether a number represented in base 10 is even? How can you quickly tell whether a number represented in base 2 is even?
