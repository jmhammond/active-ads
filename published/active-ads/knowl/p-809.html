  2. Given a graph with vertex set \(\{1, 2, 3, 4, 5\}\text{,}\) the group of automorphisms of that graph will be a subgroup of the group \(S_4\text{.}\) For each of the following subgroups of \(S_5\text{,}\) draw a graph (directed or undirected) that has that subgroup as its automorphism group.
    1. \(\displaystyle \{i\}\)
    2. The cyclic subgroup generated by \((1,2)\)
    3. The cyclic subgroup generated by \((1,2,3,4,5)\)
    4. \(\displaystyle \{\sigma \in S_5 \mid \sigma(5)=5 \}\)
  3. There are subgroups of \(S_n\) that are not the automorphism group of of any graph with \(n\) vertices. Illustrate this fact by showing that it is true for \(S_4\text{.}\) However, for any such subgroup, \(H\text{,}\) there are graphs with a larger number of vertices that have automorphism groups that are isomorphic to \(H\text{.}\) Illustrate this fact with the subgroup you identified in \(S_4\text{.}\)
