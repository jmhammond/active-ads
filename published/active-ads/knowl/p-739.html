Consider the lattices of divisors of 6, 12, 16, and 30, with the partial ordering “divides”.
  1. Determine which elements of \(D_6\) have a complement.
  2. Determine which elements of \(D_{12}\) have a complement.
  3. Determine which elements of \(D_{16}\) have a complement.
  4. Determine which elements of \(D_{30}\) have a complement.
  5. Given that \(D_n\) is always a distributive lattice, for what values of \(n\) do you suppose \(D_n\) is a boolean algebra?
